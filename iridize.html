Job: Lead Frontend Developer

Iridize

Israel

Passionate about creating beautifully simple UX for excitingly complex applications?

Based in Israel, Iridize is a personalized, user-specific onboarding optimization platform that helps companies improve their new user onboarding process, increase their conversion rates, and lower their support and training costs.

As our Lead Frontend Developer you will be in charge of developing a smooth and enticing user experience that is used by hundreds of thousands of our customers' end-users, as well as the sleek and feature-rich dashboard used by our customers for creating and managing their guides.

Job requirements:

  • Highly motivated, quick to learn and open minded
  • Passionate about creating a beautiful user experience
  • Strong JavaScript and CSS experience
  • Bonus: Experience with AngularJS, KnockoutJS
  • Even better: Experience working with Django

Are you up to it? Send your resume to team@iridize.com!
